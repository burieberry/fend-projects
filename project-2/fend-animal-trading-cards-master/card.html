Pug

The pet Pugs were highly valued by Chinese Emperors, and the royal dogs were kept in luxury and guarded by soldiers.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Height: 12 in. (male), 10 in. (female)
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Origin: China

A breed of dog with a wrinkly, short-muzzled face and curled tail. The breed has a fine, glossy coat that comes in a variety of colours, most often fawn or black, and a compact square body with well-developed muscles.
